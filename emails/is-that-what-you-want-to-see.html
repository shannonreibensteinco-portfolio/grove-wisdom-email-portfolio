It happened again, right on time. That singular moment came yesterday, on the winter solstice, where day and night were in perfect balance and tipped us into winter.

How do you feel about winter? Is is a season of rest and kindness, or weariness and stormy anxiety?

The sun is the single most important source of life for our planet. It's no wonder light has such an affect on us.

We know in our bones that it's presence is photosynthesis and life and it's absence causes anxiety and even fear.

Will it come again? Will there be enough?

In the darkness, when hope feels absent and kindness feels diminished, the solstice has reminded us every year, and right on time, look to the light.

Life will come again to the earth. Wait, rest, and look to the light.
